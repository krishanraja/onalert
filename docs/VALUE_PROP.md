# Value Proposition

## One-Liner

**OnAlert monitors government appointment systems 24/7 and alerts you within minutes when a slot opens -- so you book it before anyone else.**

## The Problem

> "I was conditionally approved for Global Entry 3 months ago. The nearest appointment is 8 months away. I've been manually refreshing the scheduler every day hoping to catch a cancellation."

This is the reality for millions of Trusted Traveler Program applicants:

- **3-12 month wait** at popular enrollment centers (JFK, LAX, SFO, ORD)
- **Cancellations are invisible** -- CBP offers zero native notification capability
- **Slots fill in 5-15 minutes** -- manual checking is a losing game
- **High opportunity cost** -- hours spent refreshing vs. living your life
- **Upcoming trip pressure** -- every week without an appointment is a week closer to traveling without Global Entry

## The Solution

OnAlert turns months of anxious refreshing into a single 2-minute setup:

1. **Set it and forget it**: Pick your program and locations, and your monitor runs 24/7
2. **12x faster than free**: Paid plans check every 5 minutes across all your locations
3. **Instant delivery**: Email alert within seconds of slot detection
4. **One-tap booking**: Direct link to the CBP scheduler -- no extra steps
5. **Real-time in-app feed**: See alerts the moment they happen, with haptic feedback on mobile
